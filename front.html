Ride Shotty! Rides on your time

Ride Shotty! allows ride-seekers to match up with available drivers, either on an immediate “Pick me Up NOW” basis or via regular, pre-scheduled transportation route matching i.e. “Pick me Up LATER”.

Learn more about Ride Shotty! - goes to Landing Page»

Benefits

Ride Shotty! takes the anonymity out of the ride sharing process and brings drivers and riders together using intelligent matching. Ride Shotty! uses social media and local organization connections to create a community of safe, reliable drivers and passengers.

Link to Landing Page

What's the catch?

There is none! For ride-seekers, all Ride Shotty! needs is your basic profile information, where and when you plan to travel. For drivers, we enable you to pre-plan your routes or offer immediate pick-up availability.

Link to Landing Page

What do I need to get started?

About 5 minutes of your time to build your profile and register with Ride Shotty! For prospective drivers, we may need a little more of your time to get some information.
